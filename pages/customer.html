FAQ

1. How long does shipping take?

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days. Standard delivery usually takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. You will receive a tracking number once your order has been dispatched.

2. Does my ERA watch come with a warranty?

Yes. All ERA watches come with a limited warranty covering manufacturing defects under normal use. Please refer to our Warranty page for full details.

3. Are ERA watches water-resistant?

Most ERA watches are water-resistant. The level of water resistance (e.g., 3ATM, 5ATM, 10ATM) is specified on the product page. We recommend checking your model’s specifications before exposure to water.

4. Can I return or exchange my watch?

Yes. We offer a return or exchange policy within 14 days of delivery, provided the watch is unused and in its original packaging. Please contact our support team to begin the return process.

5. How do I care for my ERA watch?

To maintain your watch, avoid extreme temperatures, strong impacts, and exposure to chemicals. Clean your watch gently with a soft cloth and store it in a dry place when not in use.

Warranty Coverage

Every ERA watch comes with a 2-year limited warranty, covering manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.

The warranty does not cover:

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Accidental damage or misuse
  • Damage caused by unauthorized repairs
  • Battery replacement (unless stated otherwise)

Repairs & Servicing

If your ERA watch requires servicing or repair, our dedicated support team is here to help. We recommend only authorized ERA service centers to ensure your watch receives the care it deserves.

To request a repair:

  • Contact our support team

  • Follow the return instructions provided
